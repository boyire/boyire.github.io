This site is a project notebook: a place to collect prototypes, design notes, engineering experiments, and retrospectives without publishing personal contact details.

Focus

Small, working prototypes that explore AI, frontend interaction, toolmaking, and practical product ideas.

Updates

Future entries will cover project decisions, implementation notes, visual experiments, and lessons learned during development.

Status

Projects remain private or local until they are ready to be deployed and linked publicly.
